# NLP Data Statement

The intended corpus language is British English, with possible quoted or embedded material in other languages. The publisher controls topic and genre composition. The data represents public organisational communication on one website, not the language or beliefs of all members, supporters, voters or political organisations. Automatic linguistic and multimodal models may be mismatched to politically coded, adversarial, ironic or image-heavy discourse. All high-impact interpretations require contextual human validation.
