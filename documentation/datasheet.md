# Datasheet for the Patriotic Alternative public-news corpus

**Status:** populated automatically only after collection; substantive analysis is pending execution.

- **Source population:** public news articles linked from the site's `/news` archive within the executed collection window.
- **Unit:** one article record, linked to zero or more article-embedded image records.
- **Excluded:** comments, account pages, forms, donation flows, off-domain pages and non-allowlisted image origins.
- **Layers:** immutable raw HTML/image objects; derived article text and metadata; non-reconstructive analysis outputs.
- **Personal data:** email addresses are redacted in derived text; names and public bylines require a necessity and retention review.
- **Known limitations:** publisher-selected content; deleted/inaccessible pages absent; extraction errors possible; archive and genre effects; no claims about supporters or individuals.
- **Distribution:** raw HTML, article text and images are not cleared for public redistribution. Share code, schemas, manifests, hashes and lawful aggregate derivatives by default.
- **Prohibited uses:** personal dossiers, identity inference, harassment, demographic inference, or automated high-impact decisions.
